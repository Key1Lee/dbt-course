# Airbnb and Apple Pay learning pipeline

Welcome to the dbt learning project. The original Airbnb branch transforms raw
Snowflake listings, hosts, and reviews. The Apple Pay branch starts with safe
synthetic seeds and demonstrates staging models, three dimensions, an
incremental transaction fact, a reporting mart, and data-quality tests.
